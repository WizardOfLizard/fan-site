Avatar: The Last Airbender is popular TV show by Nickelodean. It tells the story of four different nations, each with ability to 'bend' one of the four classical elements (Water, Earth, Fire, Air). The show tells the story of a boy named Aang, who is the Avatar, the only person who can bend all four elements. He must stop the Fire nation from taking over the world. He is helped by various friends that he makes along the way as he looks for people to teach him the various elements.
